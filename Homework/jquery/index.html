THE BACARDÍ MOJITO

The Mojito Cocktail has been around in one form or another since the 1500s. From famous explorers and Ernest Hemingway, to Bond and BACARDÍ, many people have played a part in the drinks long and surprising history.

What you need

  • Bacardi Superior Rum
  • 4 Lime Wedges
  • 12 fresh Mint Leaves
  • 2 tsp extra fine sugar
  • 1 Part Soda Water/ Club Soda
  • Spring of fresh mint

Steps

  1. Step 1: Take the lime wedges and squeeze them in the glass. Gently press together the limes & sugar
  2. Step 2: Bruise the mint leaves by clapping them between your palms, rub them on the rim of the glass and drop them in.
  3. Step 3: Next, half fill the glass with crushed ice, add the BACARDÍ Superiorn rum & stir.
  4. Step 4: Top with crushed ice, a sprig of mint and club soda.
  5. Mix it all together and enjoy!
